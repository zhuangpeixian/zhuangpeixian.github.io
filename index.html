Peixian Zhuang (庄培显)

I am IEEE Senior Member, CAAI, CCF and CSIG Member. Currently, I am an Associate Professor in University of Science and Technology Beijing. From 2020 to 2022, I was the Postdoctoral Fellow from the Department of Automation, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Qionghai Dai (戴琼海院士、CAAI理事长、清华大学信息学院院长). In 2016, I received Ph.D. degree from the School of Information Science and Engineering, Xiamen University, advised by Prof. Xinghao Ding. My research interests focus on underwater image processing, non-parameteric Bayesian modeling, image sparse representation, and deep neural networks.
